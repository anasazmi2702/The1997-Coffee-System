Discover our journey

Allow the smooth taste of our coffee to accompany you as you immerse yourself in the pages of our coffee tale

Since its establishment on October 21, 2021, our journey has begun beneath the warm sun, accompanied by our humble white tent, stationed by the roadside on Jalan Taman Keladi. We feel truly fortunate to have received unwavering support from our valued customers. We are delighted that our small coffee stall has enabled us to connect with numerous customers, extending beyond our circle of friends and close relatives. Surprisingly, we have even had the joy of reconnecting with long-lost friends from years gone by.

As we face challenging weather conditions, including heavy rain and changing seasons, we often ponder whether we can continue operating our shop as usual. The struggle of operating a stall by the roadside lies in our inability to foresee the weather's whims. We may encounter scorching heat, strong wind, or chilly temperatures. Either way, by the stroke of luck, we may experience operating in pleasant weather throughout the day.

Nevertheless, from the day we embarked on this business, we have never regretted the moments we have shared, the valuable experiences we have gained, and the journey we have traversed with our humble coffee stall. We remain deeply grateful for all that it has bestowed upon us, and we anticipate that the future holds even sweeter moments to balance out life's bitterness, much like a cup of coffee.
